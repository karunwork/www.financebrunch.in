🌟 About Finance Brunch

Finance Brunch is a trusted and registered financial advisory firm, dedicated to empowering clients across India, the USA, the UK, and Canada with strategic wealth management solutions. Founded by Saikarun, a finance MBA with 7+ years of market expertise, we specialize in comprehensive financial planning, tax optimization, and investment strategies designed to secure long-term financial success.

Our team comprises highly qualified Chartered Accountants (CAs) and Certified Financial Planners (CFPs), ensuring that every client receives expert, in-depth financial guidance. From mutual fund portfolio optimization to retirement planning, health & term insurance solutions, and stock market strategies, we provide data-driven, precision-focused solutions to help our clients achieve their financial goals.

As pioneers in FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) calculations, we have helped individuals and families build their retirement corpus and achieve financial freedom with strategic wealth accumulation plans.

Global Expertise & Expanding Services

With 120+ satisfied clients worldwide—and counting, Finance Brunch has a proven track record of resolving complex financial challenges. Our expert CAs specialize in IRS compliance, providing seamless tax filing and financial planning for US-based clients. Additionally, we have successfully assisted numerous international students in the USA, helping them secure better tax quotations and financial structuring.

Recognizing the growing need for holistic financial and legal solutions, we are also set to launch our dedicated legal services for US-based clients, further expanding our commitment to comprehensive financial well-being.

At Finance Brunch, we don’t just manage finances—we provide tailored strategies, expert guidance, and long-term financial security. With a licensed and highly experienced team, we are here to help you navigate wealth creation with confidence and precision.
