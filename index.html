🌟 About Us

Finance Brunch is a premier financial advisory firm dedicated to empowering individuals with strategic financial planning and investment solutions. Founded by Saikarun, a finance MBA with 7+ years of experience, we serve clients across India, the USA, the UK, and Canada, helping them navigate the complexities of personal finance.

Our expertise spans individual financial planning, stock market investments, mutual funds, health & term insurance, early retirement planning, and precise financial calculations. We specialize in FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) number calculations, helping clients determine the wealth required for early and stress-free retirement.

Backed by ARN/CFP credentials and NISM certification, we provide data-driven insights tailored to unique financial goals. As we continue to expand, we are serving clients across various terrains and financial backgrounds, ensuring that financial growth is accessible to all.
